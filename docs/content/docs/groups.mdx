---
title: Groups
description: Organize devices into groups for targeted notifications.
---

## Creating Groups

1. Go to **TailSignal > Groups**
2. Click **Create Group**
3. Enter a **Name** and optional **Description**
4. Select devices to include (use search to filter, select all/deselect all)
5. Click **Save Group**

## Editing Groups

Click the edit button on any group to modify its name, description, or device membership.

## Sending to Groups

When composing a notification on the **Send** page:

1. Select the **Group** target type
2. Choose the group from the dropdown
3. Only devices in that group will receive the notification

## Deleting Groups

Click delete on a group to remove it. This does not delete the devices — they remain registered and can be added to other groups.

## Use Cases

- **Beta Testers** — send pre-release announcements to a small group
- **VIP Users** — exclusive content notifications
- **Regional** — target devices by geography
- **Platform-specific** — group by iOS or Android for platform-specific messaging
