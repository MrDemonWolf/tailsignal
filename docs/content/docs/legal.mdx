## Third-Party Services

TailSignal uses the following third-party services:

### Expo Push Service

Notifications are sent through the [Expo Push Service](https://docs.expo.dev/push-notifications/overview/). Your Expo push tokens and notification content are transmitted to Expo's servers for delivery. Review [Expo's Terms of Service](https://expo.dev/terms) and [Privacy Policy](https://expo.dev/privacy).

### ctwillie/expo-server-sdk-php

TailSignal uses the [expo-server-sdk-php](https://github.com/ctwillie/expo-server-sdk-php) library to communicate with the Expo Push API.

TailSignal stores device information (Expo push tokens, device model, OS version, etc.) in your WordPress database. This data never leaves your server except when sending notifications through the Expo Push Service.
